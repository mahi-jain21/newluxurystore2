"To strip away the noise is to leave no room for error."

OBSCUR was conceived not as a device for measuring time, but as an exploration of physical presence. The architectural silhouette, carved from a single block of aerospace grade-5 titanium, honors the pure geometry of modern structural form. Every angle, finish, and micro-component was designed and refined at a 1:1 scale over three years of studio iteration.

Our finishing process demands seventy-two hours of manual sandblasting and diamond-tipping. The result is a tactile velvet matte texture that absorbs light, presenting a silhouette that does not demand attention, but commands it. It is a quiet performance, limited strictly to five hundred numbered pieces.
